## The brief

A drone cinematography studio needed a presence that *looks* like the work they sell —
full-bleed aerial footage, a clear service breakdown, and a fast path to an inquiry — without
taking on hosting cost or a CMS to maintain.

## What I built

- A **cinematic landing page** led by a full-bleed aerial reel, with the studio's services
  (aerial filming, post-production, delivery) broken out for film, TV, tourism, and
  commercial clients.
- A **static Next.js export** deployed to GitHub Pages — no server, no monthly bill, and
  nothing to patch.
- A **responsive layout** tuned for the pitch context: clients often open the site on a phone
  mid-conversation, so the reel and call-to-action stay legible and fast on mobile.

## Why it matters

This is client-delivery work: the engineering goal was a premium-feeling site that loads
quickly on a phone and routes a visitor to "get in touch" with minimal friction — delivered
on a budget that made a static export the right call over a heavier stack.
